Class Information

Lovelace Math's curriculum was developed by a group of talented high school students with the aim of providing quality online math education that would positively impact girls worldwide, aged 8-14.

Competition Algebra

Teacher: Sargam Mondal, 10th grader at Middlesex County Academy for Science, Mathematics, & Engineering Technologies.

Prerequisites: some background in Pre-Algebra

In this class, students will delve deeper into the fascinating world of algebra and learn to hone their analytical abilities. They will apply common algebra topics to competitive math questions, strengthening their mathematical reasoning and problem-solving skills.

Class topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Proportion and rate problems
  • Quadratics
  • Special factorizations
  • Complex numbers
  • Sequences & Series
