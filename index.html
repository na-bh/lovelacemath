About Us

Enrolling your child in a Lovelace class has several benefits:

  • Increased confidence in the classroom
  • Exposure to competition math at a young age
  • Development of problem solving skills

The Lovelace Math Circle is dedicated to help bridge the gender gap in STEM fields, particularly mathematics. We hope to encourage girls to not only exceed at the level of mathematics offered at their schools, but to excel at math competitions, a community in which girls are heavily underrepresented in. At the Lovelace Math Circle, we hope to help others realize the true beauty of mathematics by offering free, virtual math classes for girls in grades 4-9.

Frequently Asked Questions

What class should I put my child in?

Check out our "Class Information" page which has details on prerequisites and topics for each class. Choose a class based on what you would like your child to learn, and what they meet the prerequisites for.

How do I know if my child is ready to try a Competition Math class?

Our sign-up form has a problem set attached meant to assess a child's readiness for competition math classes. Have your child attempt the problems, and if they are generally successful they can enroll in our comp math classes.

Can I put my daughter in all the classes?

All the classes are at separate times, so it is possible to enroll in multiple at once. However, it may be difficult to keep up with the homework and material from multiple classes, so we recommend sticking to 1-2 classes.

What if my child misses a class?

Our instructors always post the class material afterwards, and some classes have recordings posted to our Youtube. Your child can review these resources to catch up to speed.
